What is the “Experience” section all about?

Storytelling is a wonderful way to share our life’s unique journey.

"Experiences" is a place to easily capture a memory and create a story using images, dates, location and people whom were present.

It can be used as a tool to capture a treasured document, family secret recipe, significant time or place in life or an important object which you can build a story around for posterity.

By sharing experiences, we have the opportunity to connect, understand and feel closer to each other’s journey.
